About Smart Access

Smart Access Typing & Photocopying Center has been serving the Abu Dhabi community for over 10 years. Located in the heart of Mohamed Bin Zayed City, we pride ourselves on providing fast, reliable, and professional services.

Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to helping you with all your documentation needs, from simple photocopying to complex government document processing.

We understand the importance of accuracy and timeliness when it comes to official paperwork, and we strive to exceed our customers' expectations with every service we provide.
